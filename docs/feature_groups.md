## Legend

- **🚩 leaky** — contains post-transaction balances (`newbalance*`, `errorBalance*`, `orig_drained`) that near-deterministically encode the label and are **unknown at authorization time**. Kept only as an upper-bound reference; excluded from the deployable bundle.
